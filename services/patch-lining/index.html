    Frequently Asked Questions

    Patch lining is an advanced no-dig repair technique that repairs damaged sections of drain pipes without the need for full excavation, minimizing disruption to your property.

    Patch lining costs vary based on the extent of damage and pipe location. Contact us for a free site assessment and detailed quote.

    Patch lining repairs are designed to be long-lasting, typically lasting 10-50 years depending on conditions. We provide a warranty on completed work.

    Patch lining can repair cracks, holes, joint displacement, and localized damage in drain pipes without full excavation.
