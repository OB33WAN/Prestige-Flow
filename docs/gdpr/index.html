UK GDPR Information

Last updated: 19 March 2026

This page summarises your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018 when Prestige Flow processes your personal data.

Your Data Rights

  • Right of access to personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure in certain circumstances.
  • Right to restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to data portability where processing is based on consent or contract and carried out by automated means.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time where consent is the lawful basis.

Submitting a Request

Email info@prestigeflow.co.uk with your name, contact details, and the right you wish to exercise. We may ask for proof of identity before processing your request.

Response Times

We usually respond within one month of receiving a valid request. If a request is complex, we may extend this period as permitted by law and will notify you.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with our response, you can raise a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk.

Further Information

See our full Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy for additional detail on data handling and website technologies.
