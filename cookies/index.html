Cookie Policy

Last updated: 19 March 2026

This policy explains how Prestige Flow uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, and understand usage patterns.

Cookie Categories We Use

  • Strictly necessary: required for core site functionality, security, and form submission.
  • Performance/analytics: helps us understand page usage and improve service quality.
  • Functionality: remembers preferences such as cookie choices.
  • Third-party service cookies: may be set by integrated services such as Stripe checkout and embedded tools.

How Long Cookies Last

  • Session cookies: deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: remain for a defined period unless deleted earlier in browser settings.

Consent and Control

Where required, we request consent before placing non-essential cookies. You can change or withdraw consent by adjusting browser settings and any cookie controls made available on the site.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow blocking or deleting cookies. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality, booking flow, or checkout performance.

Third-Party Services

When you proceed to secure payment, Stripe may set cookies according to Stripe's own policies. We encourage reviewing third-party privacy and cookie notices directly.
